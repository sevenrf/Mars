About the Planet

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun. Its mass is 6.39E23 kg. Atmospheric pressure on Mars is 6.01 mbar, and the level of oxygen in it reaches only 0.145%. Temperatures are reached from -153 to +35 degrees Celsius. One year on Mars is equal to 687 days
